Events

    Software Freedom Festival

    Software Freedom Festival is an event that showcases ideas and innovations based on solving real-world problems. It brings together creative thinkers, developers, and innovators to pitch, design, and build impactful solutions.

    Round 1: Idea Pitch

    Participants present their innovative ideas to tackle real-world challenges. This round focuses on creativity, clarity, and potential impact.

    Round 2: Model Craft

    Teams transform their ideas into models or prototypes, completing at least 50% of their project. Focus is on technical execution and design.

    Round 3: The Final Round

    The complete project (100%) is presented with live demonstrations, showcasing functionality and the final output to judges and audience.
